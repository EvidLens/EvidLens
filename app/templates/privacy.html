Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 26, 2026 | Effective Date: May 1, 2026

EvidLens Ltd, "EvidLens", "we", "us" is committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019 and GDPR principles.

1. Data Controller

EvidLens Ltd, Kimathi House, 2nd Floor, Nyeri, 10100, Kenya. Email: dpo@evidlens.co.ke. We are registered with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner, Kenya.

2. Data We Collect

Personal Data: Name, Email, Phone Number, County, Business Sector. Collected during signup and M-Pesa payment.

Usage Data: IP Address, Device type, Pages visited, Search queries, Reports downloaded, AI chat prompts.

Payment Data: M-Pesa Transaction ID, Amount, Date. We do NOT store M-Pesa PIN. Payments are processed by Safaricom PLC via Daraja API.

Third-Party Data: We ingest anonymized public data from NewsAPI, X/Twitter API, and AIT to generate market insights. This data does not identify individuals.

3. Legal Basis for Processing - DPA 2019 Section 25

We process your data based on: Consent, Contract Performance for subscriptions, Legal Obligation for tax records, and Legitimate Interest to improve our AI models.

4. How We Use Your Data

  1. Provide the 9-Lane Decision Intelligence Platform
  2. Process M-Pesa subscriptions and issue receipts
  3. Generate personalized AI insights using Groq LLM
  4. Send product updates, invoices, and security alerts via Resend Email
  5. Prevent fraud and comply with Kenyan law

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.

5. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored on encrypted Render Postgres servers in South Africa. Backups are encrypted at rest with AES-256. Access is restricted to 2 staff with 2FA. We retain subscription data for 7 years for KRA compliance. Free tier search data is deleted after 90 days of inactivity.

6. Your Rights Under DPA 2019

You have the right to: Access, Rectify, Erase, Object to processing, Data Portability, and Withdraw Consent. To exercise rights email dpo@evidlens.co.ke. We will respond within 21 days.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies for login and analytics cookies to improve the dashboard. You can disable non-essential cookies in your browser.

8. Third Party Processors

We share data with: Safaricom for M-Pesa, Groq Inc for AI, NewsAPI for news, X Corp for tweets. All have DPAs with us.

9. Changes

We will notify you 30 days before any material changes via email.
